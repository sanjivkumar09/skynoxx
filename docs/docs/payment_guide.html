👨‍💼 Admin Payment Workflow

Step 1: Access Payment Management

1 Login as admin and navigate to:
Payment Management Dashboard

Step 2: View Payment Statistics

2 Dashboard shows:
  • 💰 Total Revenue Collected
  • ✅ Completed Payments Count
  • ⏳ Pending Verifications
  • 🏆 Total Prizes Distributed

Step 3: Verify Player Payments

3 For each pending payment:
  1. Click "Update Payment" button
  2. View payment screenshot in modal
  3. Verify transaction ID matches
  4. Change status to Completed or Failed
  5. Click "Update Payment" to save

Step 4: Distribute Prizes to Winners

4 After tournament ends:
  1. Click "Distribute Prize" for winner
  2. Enter prize amount (e.g., 5000)
  3. Verify player's UPI ID in modal
  4. Transfer money externally via UPI/Bank
  5. Click "Distribute Prize" to record
⚠️ Important: Prize distribution in the system only records the action. You must manually transfer money to the player's UPI ID or bank account.

👤 Player Payment Workflow

1 Player joins tournament: Registration created with Pending payment status
2 Player clicks "Pay Now": Redirected to payment submission page
3 Player makes payment:
  • Pays via UPI/Card/Net Banking
  • Takes screenshot of confirmation
  • Notes transaction ID
4 Player submits details:
  • Selects payment method
  • Enters transaction ID
  • Uploads screenshot
  • Submits form
5 Admin verification: Admin verifies and updates status to Completed
6 Tournament access: Player can participate once payment is verified

📊 Payment Status Reference

Status Meaning Action Required
Pending Payment submitted, awaiting verification Admin: Verify payment details
Completed Payment verified and confirmed None - Player can participate
Failed Payment not received or invalid Contact player for clarification
Not Paid Player has not submitted payment yet Player: Submit payment details
